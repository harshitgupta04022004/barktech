# Strategy: Missing Privacy Policy and Legal Pages

## Objective

Legal pages + cookie consent before collecting leads at scale.

## Pages to add

- `/privacy-policy` — data collected, CRM storage, retention, contact DPO
- `/terms-of-use` — website terms, quote disclaimers
- `/cookie-policy` — GA4, chat widget cookies

## Cookie banner

- Use Cookiebot or Osano (or lightweight custom)
- Block non-essential scripts until consent (GA4, chat)

## Form consent

```
☑ I agree to Bark Technologies Privacy Policy and consent to be contacted about my inquiry.
```

## Effort

**Low** (legal review + 1 day implementation)
