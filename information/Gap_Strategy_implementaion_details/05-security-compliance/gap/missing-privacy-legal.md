# Gap: Missing Privacy Policy and Legal Pages

## Current state

- No privacy policy, terms of use, or cookie consent
- Forms collect PII (name, email, phone) without documented consent
- Google Analytics and WATI track users without cookie banner

## Impact

- Required for EU/export buyers (GDPR)
- Indian DPDP Act 2023 compliance gap
- App store / ad platform requirements

## Severity

**High** for production launch
