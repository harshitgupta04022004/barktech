# Strategy: E-Commerce "Quantity: 1" Field

## Objective

Replace with B2B-appropriate fields.

## Replace with

| Old | New |
|-----|-----|
| Quantity: 1 | **MOQ:** 1 unit (or remove if always 1) |
| — | **Lead time:** 50–60 days |
| — | **Price:** On request |
| — | **Warranty:** 1 year |

Remove quantity from hero block; capture quantity in **RFQ form** only.

## Template change

Standard product summary component — no per-page hand editing.

## Effort

**Low** (find-replace across product HTML or one CMS template)
