# Strategy: Typos and Trust-Damaging Copy

## Objective

Professional, proofread copy site-wide; annual content review process.

## Immediate fixes

1. Global find-replace: Massage → Message, delivuery → delivery, equipements → equipment
2. Rewrite homepage value proposition with technical writer or sales lead review
3. Update copyright to `© 2026 Bark Technologies`

## Process

- CMS content workflow: draft → review → publish
- Style guide: Indian English, B2B tone, avoid hype without specs
- Glossary: consistent terms (die-cutting, creasing, flute laminator)

## Success criteria

- Zero known typos in customer-facing copy
- Lighthouse/accessibility text review pass

## Effort

**Low** (1–2 days)
