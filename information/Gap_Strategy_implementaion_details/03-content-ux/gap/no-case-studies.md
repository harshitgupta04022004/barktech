- Generic "About Us" claims without named clients
- Blog/news section has limited install stories
- No testimonials with company names, logos, or metrics
- No certification badges (ISO, CE) displayed prominently

## Impact

- Harder to win against established brands
- Procurement teams need proof of installs in their region

## Severity

**High** for B2B conversion
