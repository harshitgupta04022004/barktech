# Gap: E-Commerce "Quantity: 1" Field

## Current state

Product pages display **"Quantity: 1"** in the product summary block — language from consumer e-commerce templates, not industrial capital equipment quoting.

## Evidence

```html
<li><b>Quantity :</b> 1</li>
```

On pages like `automatic-die-cutting-and-creasing-machine.html`.

## Impact

- Looks unprofessional to procurement teams
- Implies fixed single-unit purchase vs negotiated fleet orders

## Severity

**Medium**
