# Strategy: No Custom 404 Page

## Objective

Branded 404 that recovers traffic.

## Content

- "Page not found"
- Search box or link to **All Products**
- Top 5 popular machines
- Contact / WhatsApp CTA

## Technical

- Next.js `not-found.tsx`
- Log 404 URLs to analytics — fix top offenders weekly

## Redirects

- `quick-buy.html` → `/contact` or remove
- All legacy `.html` → clean URLs (301)

## Effort

**Low** (2 hours)
