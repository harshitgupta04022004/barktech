# Strategy: No Email Marketing Automation

## Objective

Automated nurture for long B2B sales cycles.

## Sequences

| Trigger | Emails |
|---------|--------|
| RFQ submitted | Instant ack + "what happens next" |
| Datasheet download | Day 3: case study; Day 7: book demo call |
| Newsletter signup | Monthly product news (blog CMS) |

## Tools

- Zoho Campaigns (with CRM) or Mailchimp + Zapier
- Transactional: Resend or SendGrid for RFQ ack

## Compliance

- Double opt-in for newsletter
- Unsubscribe in every marketing email

## Effort

**Low–Medium**
