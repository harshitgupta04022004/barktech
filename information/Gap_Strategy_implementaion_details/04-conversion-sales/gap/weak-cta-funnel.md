# Gap: Weak CTA and Conversion Funnel

## Current state

- Primary CTAs: "Call Now", "Request To Call Back"
- No staged funnel (awareness → consideration → quote)
- No product-specific quote buttons with pre-filled context

## Impact

- High bounce from buyers not ready to call
- No lead nurturing for long sales cycles (50–60 day delivery)

## Severity

**High**
