# Gap: No Structured RFQ Workflow

## Current state

- No quote builder, quantity field, delivery location, or application type
- Price always "On Request*" with no follow-up automation
- No quote status for customer ("received", "in review", "sent")

## Impact

- Cannot compete with configurators/RFQ flows on modern B2B sites
- Sales manually reconstructs requirements from free-text forms

## Severity

**High**
